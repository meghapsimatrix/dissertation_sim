Codebook Supplement 1.csv:
EffectsizeID… unique identifier for every effect size.

StudyID… unique identifier for every study.

Name_year… author names and year.

Article_num… unique identifier for every publication.

Items…were coded as a factor with (1) Paintings including mostly impressionistic paintings of different artists, (2) Naturalistic photographs such as pictures of birds, butterflies, (3) Artificial pictures, that is, pictures of artificial objects or creatures, (4) Mathematical tasks, such as calculating the volume of geometric solids or the use of significance tests, (5) Expository Texts, which included plain expository texts and combinations of texts and other media in a multimedia/interactive media environment, (6) Words, such as names that belonged to different conceptual categories, pronunciation rules, or translations in different languages, (7) Tastes such as liquids with different tastes.

g… Hedges’ g.

vg… variance of g.

Samplesize… number of participants.

Grey_Lit… journal articles and conference papers are coded as Published and theses and dissertations as Unpublished.

Design… within-participants design are coded as Within Design and between-participants design are coded as Between Design.

Students… samples including only students are coded as Pure Student and all other as Mixed 
Retention_Interval… short (< 20 min) retention intervals are coded as Short and al longer as Long.

Intentionality…intentional learning designs are coded as Intentional, incidental learning designs are coded as Incidental.

Transfer_Retention… transfer tests are coded as Transfer Items and retention tests are coded as Retention Items.

Simultaneity… designs using simultaneous presentation are coded as Simultaneous Presentation, designs using successive presentation are coded as Successive Presentation.

ZMean_within… standardized ratings of similarity within categories.

ZMean_between… standardized ratings of similarity between categories.

ZMean_complex… standardized ratings of complexity.

ZMean_fam… standardized ratings of familiarity.

ZMean_cur… standardized ratings of curiosity.

Age… mean age of participants.

Spaced… designs with spaced items (10 or 30 sec time intervals) are coded as spaced, designs with immediate succession of items (less than 2 sec) are coded as notspaced, designs with distractors between presentation of items are coded as distract and items not included in the analysis of spacing between items are coded as -1.

Regression_Analysis… studies included in Model 1, Model 2 and Model 3 are coded as Included otherwise as Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_With_Outlier… studies included in the Outlier Model are coded as Included otherwise as Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_IndependentStudies… studies used in independent studies analysis are coded as Included otherwise as Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_Students… studies used in pure students samples only analysis are coded as Included otherwise as Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_Visual… studies used in analysis of visual items only are coded as Included otherwise Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_Artifical… studies used in analysis of artificial created items only are coded as Included otherwise Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_RetentionInterval… studies used in analysis of retention interval are coded as Included otherwise Not Included.

Regression_Analysis_Simultaneity… studies used in the specific analysis of simultaneous vs. successive presentation are coded as 0 otherwise as 1. 

Regression_Analysis_Age… studies used in the analysis of mean age are coded as 0 otherwise as 1. 

Regression_Analysis_Spacing… studies used in the analysis of spacing between items are coded as 0 otherwise as 1.

Regression_Analysis_TransferRetention… studies used in the specific analysis of transfer tests vs. retention tests are coded as 0 otherwise as 1.
